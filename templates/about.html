My background

I started with computing at university. Fortunately, I chose to study Computer Science in the university of Granada (Spain), and that led me where I am right now, constantly learning about new things.

Moreover, in my search for new fields in programming, I took online courses, such as Artifitial Intelligence at the university of Stamford.

After learning all that university could teach me, I went on a trip to London in hopes of finding new challenges, projects to work on and people to learn from.
